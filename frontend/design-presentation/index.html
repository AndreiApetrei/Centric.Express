UX vs UI

UX vs UI

UX

Encompasses all aspects of the end-user’s interaction with the company, its services, and its products.It is the summation of your experience with a company’s brand, not just the experience associated with a singular product or service.

UI

It's what users interact with directly, everything they see, touch and hear within a piece of software or a website.

Fonts

Fonts

Start with a quality body font that complements the text. Try the same family, if it’s versatile.

Try superfamilies e.g. FF Meta + FF Meta Sans.

Look for comparable shapes (e.g. x-height, stroke width). Aim for intentional but not excess diﬀerence.

Mixing sans and serif is often good. Zoom to find balance issues.

Keep it simple! A couple of typefaces per project.
